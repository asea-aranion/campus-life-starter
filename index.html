Student Organizations

Here are some of the many exciting clubs and associations you can join!

Quokka Appreciation Society

Our mission is to spread awareness of the quokka and share its joy with the entire campus community.

Association for Mysterious Marine Life

We are passionate about marine biology and ecology, and we hope to increase awareness and appreciation for lesser-known species such as the dumbo octopus.

Be Like a Sloth Club

In our fast-paced modern world, we think it would benefit us all to take it slow sometimes. Come to our meetings to enjoy slow food, relaxing music, and naptime!
